How to Use Lex Buddy
Your guided tour — takes 60 seconds
01
🌱
Focus & Tree — Start Studying
Pick a subject, choose a timer preset (25 min, 45 min, 1 hr) or use stopwatch mode, then hit ▶ Start. Your tree grows with every minute you study — more time = a bigger, more vibrant tree.
02
✓
Study Tasks — Stay Organised
Add tasks under the Study Tasks tab. Tick them off as you go — each completed task earns you XP points and contributes to your tree's growth.
03
◫
Case Notes — Brief Any Case
Go to Case Notes to save case briefs in the Facts / Issue / Held / Ratio format. Tag each case to a subject, then filter and search your whole library instantly.
04
⧉
Flashcards — Drill Legal Terms
Create front-and-back flashcards for statutes, principles, or Latin maxims. Click any card to flip it and test yourself on the definition.
05
📅
Dashboard Calendar — Plan Ahead
Click any date on the Dashboard calendar to add a plan or scheduled task. Colour-coded dots show sessions, tasks, and case notes at a glance.
💡 Pro tip: Head to Settings to add your subjects, pick an aesthetic theme (Library, Botanical, Midnight…), choose your tree style, and set your display name. Your data syncs automatically via Firestore — just sign in from any device.
